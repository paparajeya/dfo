Welcome to Dispersive Flies Optimisation (DFO)

Dispersive Flies Optimisation (DFO) is a metaheuristic optimization algorithm inspired by the natural behavior of flies. DFO mimics the foraging behavior of flies, where flies disperse in search of food sources and communicate with each other to share information about good food sources.

DFO has been successfully applied to solve various optimization problems across different domains, including engineering, computer science, and economics. It is particularly known for its simplicity and efficiency in finding high-quality solutions.
